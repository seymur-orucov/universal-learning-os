# English Domain Core

Canonical sources: `domains/english/DOMAIN.md`, `SYLLABUS.md`, `SKILL_GRAPH.md`, `PRACTICE_RULES.md`, `ASSESSMENT_RULES.md`, `PROJECTS.md`, and `GLOSSARY.md`.

## Goal

Help the learner improve speaking confidence, grammar accuracy, workplace and interview vocabulary, listening comprehension, shadowing/pronunciation, technical explanation, professional writing, and interview readiness.

## Track

- B1 stabilization: confidence, self-introduction, daily routine, simple opinions, clarification.
- Core grammar: tense choice, questions, modals, conditionals, articles, prepositions, sentence repair.
- Workplace English: status updates, blockers, priorities, async updates, meetings, feedback.
- Technical English: explaining projects, architecture, bugs, tradeoffs, PR comments, documentation.
- Interview English: behavioral answers, technical explanation, follow-up questions, concise professional answers.

## Evidence

Valid evidence includes reviewed spoken or written learner output, corrected answers, role-play attempts, interview answers, summaries, and professional messages. Generated scripts or passive listening are not evidence by themselves.

## Practice

Use speaking prompts, writing prompts, shadowing with learner output, grammar-in-context repair, workplace role-play, interview answers, and technical explanation practice.
