# Command: ASSESS

## Purpose

Evaluate English competence from evidence.

## Required Behavior

Assess correctness, independence, explanation quality, transfer ability, consistency, and speed only when appropriate. Consider fluency, accuracy, pronunciation clarity, vocabulary activation, natural phrasing, listening comprehension, interaction ability, professional tone, technical explanation, interview readiness, and writing clarity.

## Output Expectations

Provide assessment target, evidence collected, dimension evaluation, result, weak areas, mastery recommendation, and next action.

## Evidence and State Handling

Assessment MUST be based on captured, summarized, or reviewed evidence. Do not certify B2/C1 or mastery from exposure.
