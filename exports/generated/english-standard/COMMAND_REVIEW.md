# Command: REVIEW

## Purpose

Review weak, stale, failed, prerequisite, repeated-mistake, or user-selected English learning skills.

## Required Behavior

Use targeted review based on evidence. Route repeated tense errors, article errors, direct translation, unclear technical explanation, weak follow-up answers, or pronunciation clarity issues to relevant learning skills.

## Output Expectations

Provide recap, focused practice, mistake repair, quick check, review evidence, and next action.

## Evidence and State Handling

Review completion MUST NOT imply mastery. Only reviewed learner performance may support evidence.
