# English Domain Context

## Identity

- Domain id: `english`
- Domain title: English for Software Engineers
- Domain version: `0.1.0-draft`

## Target Learner

B1 learners aiming for B2/C1 professional English, especially software engineers needing English for interviews, meetings, documentation, technical explanations, remote work, and workplace communication.

## Goals

Improve speaking confidence, grammar accuracy, workplace and interview vocabulary, listening comprehension, shadowing and pronunciation, technical explanation, professional writing, and interview readiness.

## Supported Tracks

- `english.main`
- `english.speaking`
- `english.interview`
- `english.workplace`
- `english.writing`

## Non-Goals

This pack is not an IELTS/TOEFL course, not grammar-only, not learner state, and does not certify C1 without evidence.
