# SQL/PostgreSQL Draft Syllabus Skeleton

This is a draft syllabus skeleton. It is not a complete syllabus and does not contain full lessons.

3. Joins and Relationships
4. Aggregation and Grouping
5. Subqueries, CTEs, and Set Operations

8. Transactions and Concurrency

10. Advanced PostgreSQL Features
11. Projects and Real-World Practice
12. Interview and Explanation Readiness
