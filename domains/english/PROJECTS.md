# English Project Placeholders

## Project Overview

This file defines concise English project categories only. It does not contain full project specifications, dialogue banks, shadowing scripts, transcripts, exercise banks, learner artifacts, or learner state.

## Project Categories

### Speaking Portfolio

- Purpose: collect reviewed speaking outputs across self-introduction, status updates, blockers, and technical explanations.
- Related learning skills: `english.speaking-confidence`, `english.self-introduction`, `english.work-status-updates`, `english.technical-explanation`
- Evidence value: may support fluency and independence evidence when outputs are captured or summarized and reviewed.

### Software Engineering Interview Portfolio

- Purpose: build reviewed behavioral and technical interview answers.
- Related learning skills: `english.interview-answers`, `english.technical-explanation`, `english.question-formation`
- Evidence value: may support interview readiness and explanation quality evidence.

### Workplace Communication Portfolio

- Purpose: practice meeting participation, blocker explanations, feedback, and status communication.
- Related learning skills: `english.meeting-participation`, `english.blocker-explanation`, `english.work-status-updates`
- Evidence value: may support transfer to workplace communication contexts.

### Technical Explanation Portfolio

- Purpose: collect clear explanations of software engineering concepts, decisions, and tradeoffs.
- Related learning skills: `english.technical-explanation`, `english.technical-vocabulary`, `english.collocations`
- Evidence value: may support M5 when explanations are reviewed and adapted to new prompts.

### Shadowing and Pronunciation Log

- Purpose: track reviewed shadowing attempts and pronunciation clarity improvements.
- Related learning skills: `english.shadowing`, `english.pronunciation-clarity`, `english.listening-main-idea`
- Evidence value: may support pronunciation and fluency evidence when attempts are captured or summarized.

### Professional Writing Portfolio

- Purpose: collect emails, chat messages, PR comments, summaries, and documentation notes.
- Related learning skills: `english.professional-writing`, `english.workplace-vocabulary`, `english.collocations`
- Evidence value: may support writing accuracy, tone, and clarity evidence.

## Normative Notes

- Artifact existence alone MUST NOT imply mastery.
- Project artifacts MAY become evidence when reviewed.
- Project progress belongs in learner state, not this domain pack.
- Projects SHOULD reference learning skills from `domains/english/SKILL_GRAPH.md`.
